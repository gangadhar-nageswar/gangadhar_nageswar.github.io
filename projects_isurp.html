Autonomous UV-disinfection robot

Mobile Robotics   Artificial Intelligence   ROS

I am currently working on making the UV-disinfection robot developed by Department of Biosciences and Bioengineering, IITB headed by prof. Ambarish Kunwar.

The project challenges us to develop autonomous system for indoor navigation, where GPS and maps from third-party cannot be used and so the robot needs to be localised itself and map its environment precisely.

I am working on the object detection and path planning algorithms. Due to COVID-19 situation, the work is focused towards software and as and when the situation permits, hardware implementation will be done.
