I am Gangadhar, a third-year undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India, majoring in Mechanical engineering and minors in Data Science and Systems and Control Engineering. I am also an undergraduate research assistant at the ARMS lab, IITB.
My interests lie in the intersection of AI and robotics, with a special inclination towards control systems, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.
I thrive to arrive at a proper explanation for everything, without taking anything for granted. Working in teams on various large-scale projects has hepled me develop communication skills and most importantly team-management skills to boost the productivity of the work.
I want to be a robotics researcher, particularly in the field of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and autonomous ground vehicles (AGVs). I belive that robotics can help humanity in a lot ways, and vigorous research and engineering can bring it all true.
